India Energy Trade Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes India's energy trade data from 1970 to 2023.
The dashboard helps visualize trends in energy imports and exports across multiple energy resources and highlights India's growing dependence on imported energy.


🎯 Problem Statement

India's increasing energy demand has made the country highly dependent on imported energy resources. Understanding historical trends in energy trade is important for analyzing energy security, planning future energy policies, and promoting sustainable energy strategies.


🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- NDAP (NITI Aayog Data Platform)


📊 Dashboard Features

- Interactive charts and visualizations
- KPI indicators for total imports and exports
- Trend analysis of energy trade (1970–2023)
- Comparison of different energy resources such as coal, crude oil, natural gas, electricity, and petroleum products
- Slicer

📈 Result

The Power BI dashboard successfully visualizes India's energy trade patterns using interactive charts and KPI indicators. The analysis shows a steady increase in energy imports over the years, highlighting India's growing dependence on external energy resources.


📚 Future Scope

The project can be further enhanced by integrating real-time datasets, renewable energy statistics, and predictive analytics to support better energy planning and policy decisions.
